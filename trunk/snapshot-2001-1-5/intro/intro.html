INTRO

Welcome to visit this homepage. This page guides you to the world of 'Z-Order'.

'Z-Order' is a homepage for my creations. First of all, who I am? My name is Zordius, this word is original from many things. One of them is actually the noun 'Z-Order'.

The word 'Z-Order' came from computer graphics, it means the order of depth from user viewpoint to infinity. By the time, many programming languages define this keyword for GUI design (sometimes as 'Z-Index') .

Because I like computer graphics designing and programming, I use part of the word as my name and the homepage name.

The other parts of this homepages is:

  • Z-ORDER: Introduce myself.
  • MYSTERY: Leads to my homepage works, programming and arts.
  • BRILLIANCE: My photo gallery, most of them are in subject: custume play (cosplay).
  • NEXUS: Links to homepages that belong my best friends and what I appreciate. You can find guestbook here, Feel free to write down your opinions.
  • DESINENCE: Ending staff, software list and media list.

This homepage is designed for the resolutions larger or same as 800 by 600. The best screen resolution is 1024 by 768. All special web multi-media effects are designed for Microsoft Internet Explorer 4.0+ or Netscape Communicator 4.0+ with JavaScript 1.2, HTML 4.0, CSS Level 2 and embeded-font technology. The page is best viewed under Netscape Communicator.

All pages embeded MIDI background music, only main menu is excluded. I hope you like these music, and I will update music sometime.

簡介

歡迎參觀本網頁, 在此容我為你介紹 "Z-Order" 這個世界.

"Z-Order" 主要是個介紹我的作品的網頁. 那麼, 我是誰呢? 我的名字叫作 Zordius , 這個自創字有很多的字源, 而 "Z-Order" 這個名詞正是其中之一.

這個字是從電腦繪圖學而來, 它代表了使用者視點至無限遠間的排列順序. 同時, 許多的程式語言也在圖形介面的設計上使用到這個關鍵字 (有的語言是用類似的字 "Z-Index" , 意義相同) .

因為我很喜歡電腦繪圖以及程式設計, 所以我截取這個字中的一部份當作我的名字, 還有這個網頁的名字.

這個網頁的其它部份如下：

  • Z-ORDER：我的自我介紹.
  • MYSTERY：我的網頁作品 / 程式作品 / 圖形特效 / 文字作品.
  • BRILLIANCE：我的照片作品, 其中大部份是 COSPLAY 的照片.
  • NEXUS：我好友的網頁還有個人欣賞的網頁的連結. 另外還有留言板, 歡迎在這邊寫下你的意見.
  • DESINENCE：結尾, 列出所有相關的軟體, 版權, 背景樂等等.

觀看本網頁最低的螢幕解析度是 800 乘 600 , 最佳解析度為 1024 乘 768 . 所有網頁上的視覺特殊效果以 JavaScript 1.2 , HTML 4.0 語法, CSS Level 2 標準, 還有字型內嵌技術製成, 並同時相容於 Microsoft Internet Explorer 4.0 以上版本, 還有Netscape Communicator 4.0 以上版本. 在 Netscape Communicator 下觀看本網頁, 可得最佳之效果.

此外, 除了主選單外的頁面各區都加上了背景音樂, 我會不定期整套地更換, 希望你會喜歡.
